Interesting Facts About Camels:

  • Camels ears are furry.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids.
  • Camels have two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

Facts About Chameleons:

  • A Chameleon's eyes can swivel 180 degrees.
  • Chameleons like warm, tropical environments like the rain forest.
  • Chameleons can grab their prey easily.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas:

  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans.
  • Cicadas lengthy life cycles may help them evade predators.
  • More than 3,000 species of cicadas exist.

Interesting Facts About Pandas:

  • Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black.
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons:

  • Pigeons can understand space and time.
  • Pigeons can find their way back to their nests, even 1300 miles away.
  • Pigeons are very good at multitasking.

Interesting Facts About Bears:

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  • Bears grieve deeply for others.
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.
